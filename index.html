About Kim

Kim Cole is a photgrapher based in Prescott, Arizona. From a young age she had a passion for capturing moments and her perspective of the world. She enjoys photographing the natural beauty of Arizona and the world, as well as the people and places that make it special. Currently, she is looking to expand her portfolio and share her work with a wider audience. She would also like to find clients who are looking for a photographer to capture their own special moments. If you are interested in working with Kim, please reach out through our contact form and she will make it her mission to get the perfect perspective on your cherished scenes.
